Key findings

  • MCTS is the most robust method (Phase 1) — zero divergence across 6 configurations
  • Vine diverges at 50k steps (out-of-distribution rewards) — unclipped synthetic rewards
  • VAE excels on HalfCheetah 50k but unstable on Walker2d 5k
  • Critic-guided improves MCTS on Walker2d (−12%) and HalfCheetah (−3%)
  • Critic warmup (20K steps) is critical — 5K insufficient leads to IQL divergence
  • A_mean_sel >> A_mean_all confirms the critic selects underexploited trajectories
